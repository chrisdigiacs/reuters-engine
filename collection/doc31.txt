If you or someone you know is experiencing a mental health crisis, find immediate support . Our services Access services Contact information Our services Counselling and Psychological Services offers short-term counselling to registered Concordia students who are in Quebec. Our services can help patients find new approaches to managing persistent mental health concerns and challenging life situations. Counselling plans — including the number of sessions and their frequency — vary according to each client’s needs. Many students make significant progress within three to five sessions. Some services go beyond the scope of our practice as a short-term counselling centre. If you require long-term care or specialized services, we will refer you to the appropriate care or service provider. We offer both virtual and in-person appointments. Please note that under Ordre des Psychologues du Québec (OPQ) guidelines, appointments are only available to students residing in Quebec. For holistic and timely support, students are encouraged to use all services at their disposal to access the mental health services they need as quickly as possible. This includes accessing off-campus services and tele-counselling lines that are covered by student association insurance policies, as well as private policies you may be covered under. For more resources, consult our mental health service list . Workshops & events Visit the Zen Dens for wellness workshops and events available to all students. Access services If you or someone you know is experiencing a crisis, please consult our list of emergency resources that can provide immediate support. Mental health intake appointments Intake appointments for mental health services are offered on a first-come, first-served basis to currently enrolled Concordia students who are living in Quebec. At an intake appointment, you will meet in-person with a mental health professional who will assess your needs and refer you to services. Appointments take place on the Sir George Williams Campus. Learn more and request an intake appointment Students currently registered with Counselling and Psychological Services If you have had an appointment with a counsellor at Concordia within the past year, please email your counsellor directly , or contact cps.info@concordia.ca to book an appointment. Cancellation policy You must cancel 24 hours in advance if you are unable to keep your appointment with us. Email your counsellor to cancel your appointment. View our full policies.
