Floods Water leaks are the leading cause of damage at the university. Building occupants should know what to do if a water leak occurs in their sector. If a water leak occurs in your area Vacate the area. Report the incident immediatly to Campus Safety and Prevention Services 514-848-3717. If safe do to so, remain outside the affected area until Campus Safety and Prevention Services arrives. Campus Safety and Prevention Services, Facilities Management and Environmental Health & Safety will asses the need to close certain sections of the building.
