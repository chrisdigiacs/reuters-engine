Get started on your EL roadmap Why create my own EL roadmap? Your personal EL roadmap is a tool to help you integrate experiential learning activities into your studies – and fully take advantage of the wide array of experiential learning opportunities offered at Concordia. When you include academic, co- or extra-curricular activities in your program, you strengthen your core competencies that will prepare you for the career of your dreams. How do I access my EL roadmap? Access your Experiential Learning (EL) roadmap . You can also access EL by logging in to the Student Hub and going to My CU Account > Experiential learning & professional development.
